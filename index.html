HOSA

Are you interested in a future career in the medical field? Would you like to meet new people and find more volunteer opportunities? HOSA meets biweekly after school on Wednesdays in Jass's room (325). The membership fee is $15 to join. As part of the club, there are two major events throughout the year: Fall Leadership Conference (FLC) and State Leadership Conference (SLC).

Interact

Fun and low-commitment volunteer club where you can get service hours for your resume/college applications while still having fun serving your community!! P.S. we have funny slideshows. There is no application to join! EXTREMELY low-committment: no dues, meetings aren't mandatory (only encouraged), and there is no hour requirement (though you need at least 10 for a varsity letter or cord).

The Titan Tribune

The Titan Tribune club runs an online newspaper. We publish articles about weekly. If you join, you can pick from a variety of different role options: you can write articles, help us advertise on social media or posters, take photographs, do management work, or help improve our website. You could also take a special role if you have talent like creating cartoons or graphic designs. The options are many!
