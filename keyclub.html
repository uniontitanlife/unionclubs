Key Club

Contact

Key Club is a service club that provides its members with opportunities to volunteer and meet new people. It looks great on college applications, and seniors get a cord for completing 10 hours during the school year. You can expect exciting new events every month, such as helping at marathons, fundraiser drives to donate food to the Clark County Food Bank, blanket sewing, card writing, and much more! Union is in Division 44, the Butterflies, along with other schools in our district. Our LTG hosts monthly divisional events, allowing for plenty of possibilities to socialize! Also, you can't forget about the District Convention (DCON), where over 2,000 Key Clubbers from Canada, Alaska, Washington, Oregon, Idaho, and California, come together every year at a weekend-long event in downtown Portland or Seattle!

More Information

No Application needed to join. Meetings are not required. Dues are also not required to participate in volunteering events but if you would like to participate in certain Key Club events, like DCON, Key Club Day South or Officer Elections, you must be paid.
