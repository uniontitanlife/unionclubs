Knowledge Bowl

Contact

Fun, fast-paced trivia club where you can compete with your friends against teams from other schools! Practices usually consist of either a trivia game to get familiar with the questions (like trivia bingo, matching, hangman, etc), or oral rounds with buzzers to practice for the real meets.

Meetings

Every Tuesdays and Thursdays after school (3:20) in Mr. Wiitanen's room (227). Though we would like you to come to both, only one practice per week is mandatory, as we understand there are many conflicts with other activities. Practices usually run until 4:30. Competitions are/were on Nov 2, Dec 7, Jan 11, and Feb 1, regional competition is on Feb 27

More Information

  • No application to join! Just let us know that you can be committed and you'll be put on a team
  • Moderate commitment, come to at least one practice a week
  • Won't be kicked out of if you have to miss one of these dates, but you will miss the main thing we have been preparing for at practices
  • $25 due to cover expensive equipment
  • Fun, non-judgemental environment where you can learn more about what you're interested in
  • Looks great on resumes/college applications
  • Exciting competitions where you can play with and against your friends
  • Always opportunities to step up and lead! (make your own trivia games, etc)
  • We do a brain joke every day, so if you like bad puns, this is the club for you!
