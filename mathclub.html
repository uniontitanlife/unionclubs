Math Olympiad

Contact

Math Team is a club that meets every week to do math problems together. Much of the math we do involves concepts that are not taught in the classroom, such as arithmetic, probability and combinations, and number theory. The purpose of our meetings is to become better problem solvers and prepare for upcoming competitions along the way. If you want to join but you are concerned about not being at the right math level, don't be! As long as you love math, you are more than welcome to join!
