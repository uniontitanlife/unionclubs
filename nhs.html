It's never too late to join, and we always welcome new members! If you're interested in Interacting, either contact one of our officers or join our Google Classroom (code is pnhjecm)! P.S. we have funny slideshows, if anything you should join for that.

Meetings

Once a month after school, starting at 3:20, in Mr. Wiitanen's room (room 227), usually on the first Monday of each month.

More Information

  • There is no application to join!
  • EXTREMELY low-committment: no dues, meetings aren't mandatory (only encouraged), and there is no hour requirement (though you need at least 10 for a varsity letter or cord)
  • Easy to join- you're instantly a member once you participate in one of our events
  • Some of our past projects include Thanksgiving sack lunches for the homeless, making cat toys for the Humane Society, holding a chess tournament fundraiser to raise money for Doctors Without Borders, and sending boxes of toys and toiletries around the world to children in need
