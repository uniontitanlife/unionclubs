NHS

Contact

National Honor Society is a group of individuals that strive to make a difference in our community. Our goal is to make our local community a better place by volunteering and hosting service projects. Some recent events we have participated in/hosted are letter writings, tree plantings, Walk and Knock, Salvation Army Bell Ringing, donation drives, and many more unique and fun community service events.

More Information

  • NHS is only joinable through applications. Applications are emailed to most English teachers and promoted on NHS and Titan Nation social media in the spring. It is a National requirement that all applicants have a 3.5 GPA or above and provide a copy of your unofficial transcript on your digital application.
  • NHS is a great way to meet new people and have fun with friends while bettering your community. Being a member of NHS is defined as a great honor and looks great on college applications. NHS can also pave a path to even greater opportunities down the road.
