At Union’s Red Cross club we provide various opportunities to make a difference in our community. You will be able to connect with different people around the community and have fun participating in events like cookie baking for Veterans, blood drives, and CPR training. By joining Red Cross, you will be able to maximize your talents and learn new skills and meet new people!
