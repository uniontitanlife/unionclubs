Scienc Olympiad

Contact

Science Olympiad is a competitive club. All year, we prepare for the Regional and State Science Olympiad Competitions. Competition is done in a teams of maximum 15 members (Union typically has one or two teams). Each member competes in 3-4 events with a partner or two. There are 23 events each year and topics range from Cell Biology to Electromagnetic Waves to Plane Building. Events could have you building something (Build event), participating hands-on in a lab (Lab event), taking a written test (Test event), or a combination of the three. Although Scioly is definitely a demanding club, you will be rewarded by getting to explore new scientific topics in depth. You know, if you’re into that.

Meetings

More Information

  • No Application.
  • Must pay dues to be an official member, and sign up for at least 3-4 events. Science Olympiad is a high commitment club, despite only meeting once a week. We expect team members to do the events they signed up for. Flake behavior is not cool and we don’t need people who aren’t going to put in the effort.
  • Although science olympiad is year round, it typically gets very busy the month before competition - expect to put in serious hours
  • Regional competition is in March this year, and State is later in the Spring. You should be completely available for the competition date!
