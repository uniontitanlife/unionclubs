STEM Academy

Contact

STEM Academy is a great opportunity to become more involved in our community. On the third Wednesday of every month (half day), we travel to the local feeder elementary schools and do fun science and engineering related activities with the kids! No previous science knowledge is required! For more information contact Paige, Josh, or Nathanael!

Meetings

Depends on the school you choose, but the actual sessions occur on the third Wednesday of the month (half day). Typically the meetings are after school and biweekly in Burris or Schmierers (upstairs 200)

More Information

  • No application needed, just contact one of the student leaders for more information
  • Relatively low-committment: Most people only go to session days and don’t go to planning meetings.
