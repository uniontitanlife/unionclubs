Titan Tribune

Website

The Titan Tribune club runs an online newspaper. We publish articles about weekly. If you join, you can pick from a variety of different role options: you can write articles, help us advertise on social media or posters, take photographs, do management work, or help improve our website. You could also take a special role if you have talent like creating cartoons or graphic designs. The options are many!

Meetings

Weekly on Thursdays, during both first and second lunch (come to whichever lunch is yours). First lunch meets in room #112, second lunch meets in room #427.

More Information

  • There is no experience needed to join the Titan Tribune, and no application. All you have to do is show up to a meeting and introduce yourself!
  • Attendance for every meeting is highly recommended. However, if you are very self-motivated then we can make an exception. There will likely be some work that you need to do outside meetings. The most important expectation is that you simply do what you say you are going to do! If you’re a reporter or photographer, for example, and you say you’re going to report at a certain school event, we are relying on you to follow through. Otherwise, we’re very flexible and understanding with busy schedules!
  • We create a fun and flexible atmosphere where you can learn all about how a newspaper operates and meet people who share your interests. EVERYONE is welcome and appreciated!!
