CLUBS AT UNION   

American Red Cross

At Union’s Red Cross club we connect with different people around the community and have fun participating in events like cookie baking for Veterans, blood drives, and CPR training.

More Info
FBLA

FBLA, which mean  Future Business Leaders of America, is a nationally recognized nonprofit organization that prepares students to become business leaders.

More Info
Interact

Fun and low-commitment volunteer club where you can get service hours for your resume/college applications while still having fun serving your community!! P.S. we have funny slideshows.

More Info
Knowledge Bowl

Fun, fast-paced trivia club where you can compete with your friends against teams from other schools! Practices usually consist of trivia games and oral rounds with buzzers to practice for the meets.

More Info
Math Olympiad

Math Team is a club that meets every week to do math problems that involves concepts not taught in the classroom, such as arithmetic, probability and combinations, and number theory.

More Info 
NHS

National Honor Society is a group of individuals that strive to make a difference in our community. Our goal is to make our local community a better place by volunteering and hosting service projects.

More Info
Science Olympiad

Science Olympiad is a competitive club. All year, we prepare for the Regional and State Science Olympiad Competitions. Competition is done in a teams of maximum 15 members with each member competing in 3-4 events.

More Info
STEM Academy

STEM Academy provides opportunites to high school students to work with elementary school kids at local schools around the area. Union students can spread their love for STEM to the next generation

More Info
Robotics

The Union Robotics Club contains a competitive team of 15 that competes in FIRST Tech Challenge robotics competition and a noncompetitive club where students can learn about robotics at their own pace.
