Unite!

Contact

Unite! is a club founded in partnership with the Bezos Family Foundation to increase student connectivity to school through increased club engagement. Unite! receives $1000 in funding from the Bezos Family Foundation annually and uses the funding to organize various community events, including a club showcase and a club match activity. If you want to add a stand-out activity to your resume and further your event planning, leadership, and organization skills, join Unite!
